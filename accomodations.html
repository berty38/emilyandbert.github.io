Accomodations

We have reserved blocks of rooms at two hotels near the wedding venue for guests who will need to stay overnight. If you would like a room, please make your reservation early, as the rooms in our blocks will be released for general sale (and the discounted group rate will no longer be available) on August 7, 2012.

Hamilton Park Hotel and Conference Center
http://www.hamiltonparkhotel.com/

This hotel is only one block away from the Park Avenue Club. Deluxe rooms (with either one king or two double beds) are available for $119 per night. The hotel also has a few junior suites (with one king bed), which are available at a rate of $169 per night. Suites can only be booked over the phone. Please call 973-301-9717, and provide the group code below.

To book a deluxe room, follow the link above to the hotel's website, enter your information in the "Check Availability" fields, and click on the "Promo Code" drop-down menu. Enter the following in the Group Code field: 3AD0NL.

Hyatt Morristown at Headquarters Plaza
http://morristown.hyatt.com

This hotel is located in downtown Morristown about four miles (or a ten minute's drive) from the Park Avenue Club. It is within walking distance of many of Morristown's shops and restaurants. Standard rooms (with either one king or two double beds) are available for $105 per night. "View" rooms and studio suites are available at a rate of $125 per night.
